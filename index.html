Frequently Asked Questions

Discover answers to commonly asked questions about solar energy, installation, maintenance, and more. Have a question that's not listed here? Contact us for personalized assistance!

1. What are the benefits of switching to solar energy?

Switching to solar energy can significantly reduce your electricity bills, decrease your carbon footprint, and increase your energy independence. It's a sustainable and renewable energy source that contributes to a cleaner environment.

2. How do solar panels work?

Solar panels convert sunlight into electricity through photovoltaic cells. When sunlight hits these cells, it creates an electric current that flows through an inverter to convert it into usable electricity for your home or business.

3. Will solar panels work on my home or business?

Solar panels can work on most homes and businesses that have a suitable roof or ground space with access to sunlight. Our team will assess your location during a consultation to determine the feasibility and efficiency of a solar installation.

4. What is the installation process like?

The installation process involves several steps, including a site assessment, design of the solar system, obtaining necessary permits, installation of panels and inverters, and finally, inspection and activation of the system. Our experienced team ensures a smooth and efficient installation.

5. How much maintenance do solar panels require?

Solar panels generally require minimal maintenance. Regular cleaning to remove dust and debris and occasional inspections are recommended to ensure optimal performance. Alwar Solar Bright also offers maintenance services to keep your system running efficiently.
